A Little Bit About Me
 Hi! My name is Doris Morales, I am 21 and I am a graphic design intern at Intercollegiate Game Network.

I started drawing at young age and it transformed over the last years into digital art and media. I am self taught on the Adobe Suite and Unity Engine. On the side of my main career, I also draw and do game design.

My goal is to get a job in creative, graphic design and be a fullstack developer. I also want to be able to become a well known artist and one day have an exhibit or showcase.
